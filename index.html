Latest News

BIW Model Academy Launch Records Impressive Occasion

BIW Model Academy Launch Records Impressive Occasion In Rivers State. Rivers State based enterpreneur and CEO BIW Model Management Agency, Belladonna Obialor, has launched the first model academy in the state. She unveiled the innovative platform called BIW Models Academy on the 7th of May in loud ceremony that witnessed the presence of notable personalities in Rivers State and across the South south region.

The multi million naira investment is the first of it's kind in the South south region and it is located at Portharcourt, off Olu Obasanjo road, Rivers state.

The glits and Glamour launch occasion of BIW Model Academy recorded impressive turnout, with the distinguished presence of Christy Egiri, Solomon Egiri, Favour Benjamin, People Roberts, Ilami Harry, Victor Jonny, Oputa, Destiny Chijioke and a host of others.

According to Belladonna Obialor, the newly established models' Academy will be a structure through which young aspiring Models and established models can be nurtured, empowered and guided in the modelling career.

The BIW Model Academy is sure to define a new pace in the paths of successfully improving the modelling agency in Rivers State and across the South south region. It's vision is to set standards, develop opportunities, create ideas and ensure that models rights are defined and preserved.
